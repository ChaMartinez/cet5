    Who we are

    JUN GLE

    CARTWHALE was born in the hands of Jun gle, with the desire to promote local products on one website. It is a space wherein we disseminate beauty and empowerment to all Filipina worldwide. Our goal is to make everyone feel confident in their skin, with just one click.


    OUR STORY

    We believe everyone deserves to feel beautiful. On good days and bad days. In big ways and quiet ways. We believe everyone deserves to face the world feeling amazing about themselves. We believe you can feel amazing through the little things. But we also understand that it's a complex and overwhelming journey. This is why we've found our purpose in being here, rooting for you, every step of the way. We love you just the way you are. But we love it most when you feel beautiful.


    Our Function

    Easy Navigation And Intuitive Operation Our online store is well laid-out and easy to use, making your shopping experience easy and convenient. We constantly try to make our website and app ever more user-friendly, and every time you come back your experience becomes more and more personalized.


    Simple And Safe Shopping Once you've picked out your items and added them to your cart, you can choose from a number of secure payment options such as Cash on Delivery (COD), direct debit, credit card, Paypal, and bank transfer. After a successful order, you will receive a tracking number that you can use to track your order. Delivery notifications are also sent via SMS to keep you updated on the current status of your order.
